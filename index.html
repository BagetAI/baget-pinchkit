THE THREE-SACHET SYSTEM

To preserve aroma and critical essential oils, we separate our spices into three distinct, chronological sachets.

01

Ground aromatic spices that sizzle in hot oil early in your prep. Formulates your core flavor foundation.

02

Whole, unground spices to simmer deep inside the liquids. Slowly extracts deep complexity.

03

FINISH & BRIGHTEN (SAGE)

Delicate herbs and finishing heat-sensitive powders stirred in during the final 2 minutes.
